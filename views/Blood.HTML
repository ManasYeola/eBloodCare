How to Donate

  • Drink extra water before your appointment.
  • Eat a healthy meal, avoid fatty foods.
  • Wear a shirt with sleeves you can roll up.
  • Let us know if you have a preferred arm or vein.
  • Relax, listen to music, or read while you donate.
